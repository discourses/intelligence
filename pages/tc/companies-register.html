DISTRIBUTION OF COMPANIES

The distribution of companies node launches a network graph. Each node's tooltip number represents a count of companies, w.r.t $$n_{c} = \Large{\sum}^{I}_{i = 1}{n_{i, c - 1}}$$ wherein $n_{c}$ denotes the number of companies of a node category, e.g., the number of members of the services to buildings and landscape activities division vis-à-vis the administrative and support service activities section.
